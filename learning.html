Learning

Our Morning Class Program is uniquely and intentionally designed for parents and children to enjoy English through play. Our lesson is filled with songs and activities that stimulate your child’s five senses, baby signs and  incorporating the right brain development. We also provide a safe and appropriate environment that encourages each child’s individual social, emotional, physical and cognitive development.

Baby Class

Target

pregnant mothers and mothers with infants from 0 to 9 months
*with parents

Date＆Time

9:00〜9:40 for 40minutes
Tuesday, Thursday

Aim

  • offer a sensory-rich environment to create and strengthen neural pathways. (such as , Baby Massage, Mama and Baby Yoga and sensory-motor play)

  • enhance mother’s bond with child and supporting growing infants’ development

 

Schedule

9:00 ～ 9:10

Warm up

9:10 ～ 9:25

Cards / Chant / Baby Sign　

9:25 ～ 9:40

Baby Massage / Yoga / Sensory Play

Toddler Class

Target

Mothers with active infants and toddlers (starting from 9 months to 24 months)

Date＆Time

9:00〜9:40 for 40minutes
Wednesday, Friday

Aim

  • enhance mother’s bond with child and supporting active infants’ and toddlers' development

  • a transition class that prepares the active toddler for our ELC Teddy Bear Class.

  • enriched with lots of fun activities such as Yoga, Baby sign and sensory motor activities.

 

Schedule

9:00 ～ 9:20 

Circle Time / Chant / Baby Sign 

9:20 ～ 9:30

Creative movement / Yoga 

9:30 ～ 9:40

Sensory Play Activities
